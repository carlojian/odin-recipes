Chicken Fried Steak

Description:

Dive into the comforting flavors of the South with this delicious Chicken Fried Steak recipe. Tenderized steak is coated in a seasoned batter and fried to golden perfection, then smothered in creamy white gravy. It's a hearty and satisfying meal that will quickly become a family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 4 beef cube steaks (about 4 ounces each)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

For the Gravy:

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prep the steaks:
    • Place the cube steaks between two sheets of plastic wrap or wax paper.
    • Use a meat mallet to pound the steaks to about 1/4-inch thickness.
    • Season each steak with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Set Up the Dredging Station:
    • In a shallow bowl, combine the flour, salt, pepper, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Mix well.
    • In another shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs and buttermilk.
  3. Coat the steaks:
    • Dip each steak into the flour mixture, ensuring it's evenly coated, and shake off any excess.
    • Next, dip it into the egg and buttermilk mixture, allowing any excess to drip off.
    • Return the steak to the flour mixture and coat it one more time, pressing the flour mixture onto the steak to adhere.
  4. Fry the Steaks:
    • In a large skillet, heat about 1/4 inch of vegetable oil over medium-high heat until it shimmers.
    • Carefully place the coated steaks into the hot oil. Fry for about 3-4 minutes per side or until they are golden brown and crispy.
    • Remove the steaks from the skillet and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.
  5. Make the Gravy:
    • In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Sprinkle in the flour and whisk continuously for 1-2 minutes to create a roux.
    • Gradually pour in the milk while whisking constantly to prevent lumps.
    • Continue to cook and whisk until the gravy thickens, about 3-5 minutes.
    • Season the gravy with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve:
    • Spoon the creamy gravy over the fried steaks.
    • Serve hot alongside your favorite sides, such as mashed potatoes, green beans, or biscuits.

Enjoy the comforting goodness of homemade Chicken Fried Steak with creamy gravy that's sure to satisfy your Southern food cravings!
