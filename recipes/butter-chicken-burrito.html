Butter Chicken Burrito

Description:

Savor the irresistible fusion of flavors with our Butter Chicken Burrito! Tender marinated chicken, simmered in a luscious tomato and cream sauce, is lovingly wrapped in a warm tortilla alongside fragrant Basmati rice and fresh cilantro. This culinary masterpiece combines the best of Indian and Mexican cuisine in one unforgettable bite. Get ready to embark on a taste adventure that will leave your palate craving more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Cooked Basmati rice

Instructions:

  1. Marinate The Chicken:
    • In a bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
    • Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss to coat.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    • In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat.
    • Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. Make the Sauce:
    • In the same skillet, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter.
    • Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until the onion is translucent and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.
    • Pour in the tomato sauce and heavy cream. Stir to combine.
    • Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken.
    • Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  4. Assemble the Burritos:
    • Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for about 20 seconds to make them pliable.
    • Place a portion of cooked Basmati rice in the center of each tortilla.
    • Spoon the butter chicken mixture over the rice.
    • Sprinkle with chopped fresh cilantro.
  5. Roll and Serve:
    • Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then roll it up tightly from the bottom to enclose the filling.
    • Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.
    • Optionally, heat the assembled burritos on a skillet for a minute or two on each side to lightly toast and seal.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve your Butter Chicken Burritos immediately with extra cilantro and a side of cooling yogurt or raita if desired.

Indulge in the harmonious blend of flavors as you savor these Butter Chicken Burritos, bringing the best of India and Mexico to your plate in one delicious bite!
